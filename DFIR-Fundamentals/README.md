## 🗺️ Topics Covered & Folder Map

### 1. [Evidence Handling & Acquisition](./01_evidence_and_acquisition.md)
* **Defining Digital Evidence:** Binary form realities and establishing probative value.
* **The Triad:** The practical differences between *Collection*, *Acquisition*, and *Preservation*.
* **Core Principles:** Maintaining evidence integrity, the write-blocker workflow, and managing volatile exceptions.
### 2. [Sources & Types of Digital Evidence](./02_evidence_sources_and_types.md)
* **Physical Sources:** Containers vs. storage devices (HDD, SSD, Volatile RAM) and network transmissions.
* **Logical Sources:** System producers (OS logs, web servers, registry hives) and data fragments.
* **The Evidence Chain:** A structural look at how physical containers map down to individual logical logs.
* **Acquisition Formats:** Quick reference for files, bit-for-bit disk images, and memory dumps.

### 3. [The Forensic Process & Objectivity](./03_the_forensic_process.md)
* **Investigation vs. Examination:** Macroscopic strategy vs. microscopic task execution.
* **The Objectivity Metric:** A matrix breakdown of how to report artifacts accurately without injecting subjective bias.
* **The Interpol Lifecycle:** The cyclical, 5-step process
* **Defensive Tactics:** A look at how forensic skills counter **MITRE ATT&CK Tactic TA0005 (Defense Evasion)**.
